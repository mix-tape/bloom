Life at Bloom

Your new home at Bloom Terraces nestles in a secure leafy enclave between city and coast on the edge of a nature sanctuary, with all the attractions of Perth’s western suburbs just minutes away.

The word Bloom elicits a sense of beauty, nature and renewal – reflecting the landscaped gardens surrounding the homes together with the nature spaces of nearby Herdsman Lake Regional Park. The best of the west is yours at Bloom Terraces, Wembley.
